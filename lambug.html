Freediving

Witness the breathtaking Sardine Run, Swim alongside majestic sea turtles, Explore the underwater world with certified freedive instructors.

Scuba Diving with Sardines

The Moalboal Sardine Run does not move and can always be found in the Panagsama beach area, providing visitors with the opportunity to see this spectacle year-round. The schools tend to be between 2-15m/7-40ft making it an enjoyable experience for scuba divers.
