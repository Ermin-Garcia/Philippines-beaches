Things To Do

in El Nido Palawan, Island of the philippines.

El-Nido

Zipline

The 750 meter long line gives you a full-fledged view of the tiny settlements by the beach below you, the coconut fringed shoreline, and the islands in the distance, all appearing in a clearer hue as you zipped closer!

Kayaking

Kayaking is that it's the ultimate beginner-friendly sport. It doesn't matter if you've never paddled anything in your life, you can enjoy and excel at kayaking. Kids, older adults, people with injuries, and even dogs can all enjoy kayaking.

Cliff Jumping

Sea Caves can be found at various locations in this area…the area of Palace Caves is great for cliff jumping or diving (make sure you aim for the bright blue sand area or you'll smash yourself on the rocks below)…the jump is about 10 meters high.
