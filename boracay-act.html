Things To Do

Boracay

Helmet Diving

Go on an amazing walk underwater with Boracay Helmet Dive experience. On this trip, you will get to stroll underwater just as you do on land. Stand on the sandy sea bed, see coral reefs, colorful exotic fishes and so much more!

Flyfish Ride

Go on an exciting full-filled water ride in Boracay Flyfish! Flyfish is an inflatable raft, similar to Banana Boat that is pulled along by a speedboat.
