Things To Do

Coron

Island Hopping

Via private Double Decker Party Boat. Experience the thrill of a full day double decker pontoon boat rental. Some of the best Island moments, Enjoyed include exploring the Barracuda and Kayangan Lake in Coron, Snorkeling the reef gardens, and Scuba diving in Coron.

Island Scuba Diving

Divers can explore the skeletal remains of four different wrecks: Okikawa Maru, Akitsushima, Olympia Maru, and East Tangat Gunboat. These wrecks provide the perfect playground for divers.

Island Snorkeling

Coron boasts several excellent snorkeling sites. The most famous ones include the Twin Lagoons, Siete Picados, and Barracuda Lake. There are also several Japanese Shipwrecks from World War || around Coron that offer fascinating snorkeling and diving experiences.
